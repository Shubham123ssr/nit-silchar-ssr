NIT SILCHAR

National Institute Of Technology, Silchar (राष्ट्रीय प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान, सिलचर) is one of the 31 National Institutes of Technology of India and was established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College in Assam. In 2002 it was upgraded to the status of National Institute of Technology and was declared as Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

OUR CAMPUS

The college is situated in the Barak valley. It is a beautiful place with an abundance of greenery and lakes all around.
The evening sunsets are a joy to watch here. The roads are clean a in good condition, Hostel is great.
Internet connectivity is more than needed. Professors and Phd students are helpful.
We have the best seniors here, They are very helpful.
Just thinking about the day when we will have to leave this college makes me feel sad.

STUDENT REVIEW

NIT Silchar in one word can be define was "heaven".Fillled with natural beauty it is one of the fastest developing NIT in India.
The faculties are extremely talanted and are extremely friendly and are ready for help at a any time.

The college have a lot of potential but due to its remote location it has suffered negligence from the rest part of the country.
Infrastructure is good. Better facilities should be there regarding research and internship.
Rather than focussing more on academics more should be given on projects, research and innovation.

Placement and Job oppurtunities is good especially in IT sector and semi govt company.
But with the PSU sector having stopped coming to NIT's , annual package for job has reduced drastically.
